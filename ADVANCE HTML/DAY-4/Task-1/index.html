Introduction

A little bit about us.

As Chairman of the Association I offer you a very warm welcome to our newly developed website.

I claim that if you have epilepsy the best place in the world to live is in the Liverpool area. This is because it is the home of two international centres of excellence for the treatment of the condition. The Walton Centre for Neurology and Neurosurgery caters for the adult population and Liverpool Alder Hey Children, hospital for the youngsters.

Our website provides information on many aspects of the condition and we will continue to update it as advances are made on all fronts.

Dr Simion Keller

Chairman and Director of MERA

Children's Section

Educational fun.

If you are child or the parent of a child with epilepsy, we know that it can be difficult to adjust to life - especially if you are recently diagnosed.

MREA are here to help to educate, inform and entertain you with the Children, Section - we have games, quizzes, an animated stort called 'Molly, Tale' and an Interactive Illustrated Junior Encyclopaedia.
